Step 2: Career Discovery Assessment

This assessment looks beyond specific technical skills to understand how you think and what drives you.

1. Which of these activities makes you lose track of time? (Select all)

2. How do you prefer to tackle a major challenge?

3. What is your ideal daily work environment?

4. What do you value most in your future career? (Select all)

5. What is one skill you are proud of (could be a hobby or academic)?

6. How do you prefer to communicate your ideas?

7. At the end of the day, what outcome makes you feel most satisfied?

8. Describe a topic or hobby you could talk about for hours.

9. Which broad sectors interest you? (Select all)

10. How many hours per week can you invest in exploring your path?
