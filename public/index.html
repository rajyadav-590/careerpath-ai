Frequently Asked Questions

Is CareerPath AI only for engineering students? +

No, CareerPath AI is designed to help students from any stream, including engineering, arts, commerce, and science. The AI models are trained on diverse career paths and skill requirements across various industries to provide relevant, personalized guidance.

Does this replace human mentors? +

Absolutely not. CareerPath AI is a powerful tool designed to augment and accelerate your career planning by providing data-driven recommendations and structured roadmaps. It should be used in conjunction with the valuable advice and personal connections offered by human mentors.

Is this a free or paid tool? +

The initial assessment and top career match summary are often offered as a free tier. Detailed roadmaps, skill gap analysis, and resume review features are part of our premium subscription. Specific pricing is available on our main site.

How accurate is the AI assessment? +

Our AI uses a combination of psychometric models, large language models (LLMs), and up-to-date market data, resulting in a high degree of accuracy (typically 85-90% match score reliability). However, it is an assistive tool, and final career decisions should always be based on personal reflection and mentorship.
